Multiple sources confirm NimbusTech was founded in 2015.

## False: NimbusTech serves over 50 million users worldwide
The evidence does not mention NimbusTech serving over 50 million users worldwide, instead discussing Project Nimbus and other unrelated topics.

## False: India's population crossed 2 billion people in 2024
**Correct fact:** India's population has crossed 1.46 billion
The claim of India's population crossing 2 billion in 2024 is directly contradicted by the evidence.

## False: NimbusTech's revenue grew 300% year-over-year
The claim is about NimbusTech, but the evidence provided is about different companies such as Nimbus Projects, Axon, Lenovo, and Alphabet.

## False: NimbusTech's revenue reached $120 million in FY2025
No credible evidence supports the claim about NimbusTech's revenue.

## Inaccurate: NimbusTech is headquartered in Bengaluru, India
**Correct fact:** NimbusTech is headquartered in Mumbai, India
Multiple sources confirm NimbusTech is based in Mumbai, not Bengaluru.

## Verified: The Eiffel Tower was completed in 1889

## False: NimbusTech's flagship product was launched on January 1, 2023
